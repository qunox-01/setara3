Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. What We Collect

We collect:

  • Contact data you submit (such as email in update forms).
  • Feedback submissions.
  • Usage analytics events and session identifiers.
  • Generated scorecard report data when you create a shareable report link.

2. CSV Uploads

Uploaded CSV/TSV files are used to run analyses. Raw files are not intended for long-term storage by default in normal analysis flows.

If you generate a report link, derived analysis results are stored to serve that report URL.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use a first-party session identifier cookie and third-party analytics technologies (including Google Analytics) to understand product usage and improve the service.

See the Cookie Policy for details.

4. Why We Use Data

We use data to operate tools, provide requested reports, improve reliability, measure engagement, and communicate product updates where requested.

5. Data Sharing

We may share data with infrastructure and analytics providers acting as service providers. We do not sell personal data.

6. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for operational, security, analytics, and legal purposes. Exact retention schedules should be defined operationally and updated here.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries different from your own, subject to applicable legal safeguards.

10. Contact

For privacy requests, use the contact channel listed on the site or repository owner profile.
